Hello everyone! I’d like to share my experience — maybe it’ll be helpful to someone.

While looking for a decent company to work with, I kept running into flashy promises like “everything will be great” or “we offer the best conditions.” But in reality — it was just disappointment. I worked with various companies — American, Russian-speaking, even big names like FedEx Freight and Kroger. Everywhere there was something wrong: dispatchers didn’t know what was going on, long wait times, low rates, and you always felt like just another number on a list.

Now I’m working with a small family-owned company — Alyonka Enterprises LLC. I’m an owner operator, and honestly, this has been the best experience in my 6 years of trucking in the U.S. Everything is straightforward here: no hidden tricks, constant communication, and respectful treatment. They mostly run dry vans, have access to Amazon loads, and work with trusted brokers.

The freight is steady, and if needed — you can be home every weekend, just like I do. They helped me with paperwork and equipment — everything was smooth, without stress or chaos. The company currently has one or two dry vans available, and they’re looking for reliable people to join the team.

I’m not getting any benefit from this, there’s no referral program — I’m just sharing because I know how hard it is to find a good company. If someone’s looking for honest and stable work — reach out, I’ll share the contact info. And if we meet at a loading dock a year from now, I won’t be ashamed to chat.

Wishing you all smooth roads and great loads!
